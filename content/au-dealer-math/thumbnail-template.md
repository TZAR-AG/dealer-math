## Template specs

- Size: 1280×720 (YouTube standard)
- Background photo: 70% of frame
- Charcoal gradient overlay: bottom 30%, 40% opacity
- Title: Inter Black 110pt, white slab-caps, 3-6 words
- Underline: outback orange `#D17A3D` hand-drawn under provocative word
- Mascot: 30% of frame, bottom-right corner (Macca character cutout from Fiverr deliverable library)
- Dollar figure (optional): Fraunces italic 80pt, outback orange `#D17A3D`, top-left

## Brand kit colors (load into Canva Settings → Brand Kit)

- Charcoal `#2B2B2B`
- Outback orange `#D17A3D`
- Cream `#F5EFE6`
- Charcoal soft `#4A4340`

## Brand kit fonts

- DM Sans Bold (display)
- Inter Black (titles)
- Fraunces (numbers)

## Per-video workflow

1. n8n picks 3 background photo candidates from Storyblocks (cars, dealership, money)
2. n8n picks Macca cutout based on script tone (neutral / alert / resigned)
3. Canva API generates 3 thumbnail variants (A + B + C)
4. Adrian picks 1 at Checkpoint #2
