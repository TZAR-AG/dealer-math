## Cadence integrity rules

1. **Promised = delivered.** Every video's "next video preview" line is binding. The next video MUST deliver that exact topic.
2. **No filler.** If we don't have a compelling next-week reveal, we don't promise one — drop the preview from the sign-off.
3. **Buffer of 2 videos minimum.** By V1 publish, V2 should be at least drafted. By V2 publish, V3 should be drafted. Always one video ahead.
4. **No script changes after publish.** Once a video is up, the "next video preview" is locked and CANNOT be changed retroactively. This forces calendar discipline.
