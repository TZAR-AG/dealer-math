## SOP per render

1. Open Artlist → Nano Banana Pro
2. **Set aspect ratio to 16:9 in UI dropdown** (mandatory — UI controls output)
3. Paste prompt 1 verbatim
4. Generate (run 4 variants per spec; pick best)
5. Save winner to `stills/v2/still-v2-2A-training-binder.png`
6. Repeat for prompts 2-10
7. Verify each render against the AUDM brand-lock checklist:
   - [ ] No human figures or reflections of people
   - [ ] Palette is charcoal/cream/outback-orange — no drift to mint/beige/olive
   - [ ] Light direction is upper-left
   - [ ] Documentary cinema feel (NOT iStock / luxury ad)
   - [ ] No readable text or logos
8. Reject + re-render if any check fails
