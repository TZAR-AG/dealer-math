# AU Dealer Math — Channel Banner v1 (placeholder, pre-mascot)

## Purpose

Pre-launch banner before the Fiverr mascot deliverable lands. Placeholder is intentional — *better than no banner* for the algo "complete profile = real channel" signal, but keeps the door open for a proper mascot-driven v2 once the illustrator delivers in 5-7 days.

Per Agent 3 research: missing banner reduces sub-conversion ~30-50%, so even a placeholder is critical before V1 publishes.

---

## Specs

- **Dimensions:** 2048 × 1152 px (YouTube standard, with safe zone in centre 1235 × 338)
- **File format:** PNG
- **Brand colours:** charcoal #2B2B2B + outback orange #D17A3D + cream #F5EFE6 (locked)
- **Fonts:** DM Sans Bold (display) + Inter Black (titles) + Fraunces (numbers/accent only)
- **Style:** clean typography, NO photo background, NO mascot characters (those land in v2 once mascot delivers)

---


### Element-by-element

**Background:**
- Solid charcoal #2B2B2B fill
- Subtle radial gradient toward outback orange #D17A3D in bottom-right (~5% opacity, almost imperceptible — adds depth without being graphic-heavy)

**Wordmark "AU DEALER MATH":**
- Inter Black 220pt
- Cream #F5EFE6
- Letter-spacing tight (-2)
- Position: centre-left within safe zone, vertically centred

**Underline:**
- Hand-drawn-style outback orange #D17A3D stroke under the wordmark
- Stroke weight 8pt
- Length: under "AU DEALER" only (not the full wordmark — partial underline draws the eye)

**Tagline:**
- *"What dealers don't tell you about the math."*
- DM Sans Regular 60pt
- Cream #F5EFE6 at 70% opacity (softer, secondary read)
- Position: directly below wordmark, 60pt vertical gap

**Cadence line:**
- *"New explainers Mon · Wed · Fri  6pm AWST"*
- Inter Regular 36pt
- Cream #F5EFE6 at 50% opacity (tertiary read, supporting info only)
- Position: bottom of safe zone

**No mascot. No images. No icons.** Pure typography.

---

## Why this works for v1

- **Algo signal:** complete profile = "real channel"
- **Brand consistency:** establishes the typography + colour palette before mascot arrives
- **Mobile-readable:** all text within 1235×338 mobile-safe centre zone
- **Easy upgrade path:** when mascot lands, swap to v2 with character cutouts on the right side, keep typography on left
