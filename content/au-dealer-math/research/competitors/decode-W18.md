## Pick 1 — Aussie Finance With Luke (sub-10K AU sibling, the scale-matched comp)

**"What To Do With $100K Saved (Most Aussies Get This Wrong)"** · [8tyrq7AAlv0](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8tyrq7AAlv0)
- **Score:** 10.4× baseline · 42K views in 5d · 8:47 long-form
- **Channel state:** 7.8K subs (closest scale comp we have — daily AU finance cadence)
- **Thumb:** Hand-drawn whiteboard cartoon. Stick-figure character with green AU-flag scarf (yellow stars), holding a money bag labeled "$$ 100,000". Blue hand-drawn marker text right side: "WHAT TO DO AFTER SAVING $100,000?" — underlined.

### Why it broke
- **Specific dollar amount** anchored on both thumb AND title ($100K) — search-discoverable
- **AU-cultural visual cue** (flag colors as scarf) — locks AU-audience targeting
- **Hand-drawn / cartoon style** = pattern interrupt vs photo-real norm in finance niche
- **Identity callout in parenthetical:** *"(Most Aussies Get This Wrong)"* — you-might-be-wrong hook
- **Daily cadence at 7.8K subs** proves the format ABSOLUTELY works at AUDM's scale

### Transfer for AUDM

| Element | What AFWL did | What AUDM should do |
|---|---|---|
| Title pattern | `[Action verb][specific $][parenthetical identity callout]` | Use this formula on V12 (Novated Lease): **"How to Get $8,400 Back from a Novated Lease (Most Aussies Get the Math Wrong)"** |
| Thumb dollar | $100,000 dominant on thumb | Always include the SPECIFIC dollar figure in the thumb (V12: "$8,400" big and bold) |
| AU cultural marker | Green/gold flag scarf | AUDM equivalent: AU plate silhouette, RBA building silhouette, AU map outline — pick one and reuse |
| Style differentiation | Hand-drawn vs photo-real | AUDM is doc-forensics photo-real (locked) — that's already a differentiator from AFWL's cartoon AND from CarEdge's face. Stay locked. |
| Parenthetical pattern | "(Most Aussies Get This Wrong)" | Steal verbatim. Use on V8, V9, V12. |

## Pick 2 — Lucky Lopez (news-jack mechanic, the BIG outlier of the week)

**"Car Part Manufacturers are Going Bankrupt"** · [mOZKgKlrvB8](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mOZKgKlrvB8)
- **Score:** 37.8× baseline · 49K views in 2d · 9:04 long-form
- **Channel state:** 522K subs · the closest narrative comp to Mac
- **Thumb:** Photo-real night exterior. Lucky standing in front of brightly-lit AutoZone storefront at night, wet pavement. Holding a chaotic overflow of car parts (turbo, intake, manifolds). Confused/surprised face. HUGE all-caps white condensed bold text top: **"NO PARTS"**.

### Why it broke
- **News-jack on a real economic event** (auto parts manufacturer bankruptcies) — already-trending search demand
- **Title states cause, thumb states EFFECT** — title says "manufacturers going bankrupt", thumb says "NO PARTS" (consequence to YOU). Self-interest hook stronger than abstract news.
- **Branded retailer in background** (AutoZone) = trust anchor, viewer recognizes the place
- **2-word punchy text** dominates ("NO PARTS") — readable on phone scrub at thumbnail size
- **Night/dramatic lighting** = serious-news register, distinguishes from daytime explainer norm

### Transfer for AUDM

| Element | What Lucky did | What AUDM should do |
|---|---|---|
| Title-thumb asymmetry | Title = WHY (mfr bankrupt). Thumb = EFFECT (NO PARTS) | V11 (Agency Model) — title: *"How the Agency Model Killed Negotiation"*. Thumb: **"PRICE IS PRICE"** (the consequence). |
| 2-3 word thumb text | "NO PARTS" — condensed bold, all-caps, top of frame | Mandatory for every V[N] thumb. Pick the punchy 2-word distillation of the consequence. AUDM examples: "DOUBLE PAID" · "RATE SPREAD" · "NO REFUND" · "FINANCE TRAP" · "ROOM #2" |
| Branded retailer anchor | AutoZone storefront | AUDM equivalent: Toyota / Holden / Mazda dealership exterior at night/dusk. Adrian's MJ swatches need to add a "branded AU dealership exterior at dusk" template. |
| News-jack format | Real economic event tied to the niche | This is gap #9 from the ultrathink — **reserve 1 weekly cadence slot for news-jack flex**. ASIC ruling, RBA rate move, auto-mfr news, Cox AU pricing report — all fuel for this format. |
| Length sweet spot | 9 minutes flat | Matches AUDM's V1-V12 lock (~9-10 min). Confirmed correct length band. |

## Pick 3 — How Money Works (faceless format mechanic, viral at scale)

**"WTF is Going On at The FED?"** · [DGLw0Y3F9uk](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DGLw0Y3F9uk)
- **Score:** 9.9× baseline · 503K views in 2d · 19:02 long-form
- **Channel state:** 1.7M subs · faceless finance · the format benchmark
- **Thumb:** Photo-real Senate hearing shot of Jay Powell (Fed Chair). Arms crossed, defiant body language, "Jay Powell" nameplate at bottom for institutional context. Marble background. HUGE all-caps white bold text top: **"I'M NOT F**KING LEAVING!"** (self-censored).

### Why it broke
- **Real public figure** = instant authority + recognition (Powell is news-cycle adjacent every week)
- **Quote-as-thumb-text** framing — text reads as if Powell *said* it, even though it's editorial
- **Self-censored profanity** = curiosity hook within YT-policy-safe ("did he REALLY say that?")
- **Title-thumb asymmetry** — title is mild question, thumb is explosive answer
- **Faceless channel format** proven viable at 1.7M subs and 503K-view spike — direct format proof for AUDM

### Transfer for AUDM

| Element | What HMW did | What AUDM should do |
|---|---|---|
| Real public figure on thumb | Jay Powell at Senate | AU equivalents Mac could borrow weekly: Michele Bullock (RBA Governor), Joe Longo (ASIC Chair), Jim Chalmers (Treasurer), or specific dealer-principal CEOs (Toby Nankervis, etc — defamation-safe with neutral framing) |
| Quote-as-thumb-text | "I'M NOT F**KING LEAVING!" | Pull a quote from the figure's actual public statement OR a paraphrase that's clearly editorial. AUDM example: RBA on car finance: **"WE'RE LOCKED IN"** (paraphrased commentary on rates) |
| Self-censored profanity | F**KING | One-time tool — overuse degrades. Reserve for ONE major news-jack video where the topic genuinely warrants it. |
| Title-thumb asymmetry | Title curious, thumb explosive | Adopt as default. Title = the question, thumb = the bombshell answer / consequence |
| Length | 19 minutes (almost 2× AUDM lock) | Note: HMW runs 15-20min on news-jack topics. AUDM's 9-10min lock is fine for non-news-jack; consider 12-15min specifically for news-jack flex slot. |
